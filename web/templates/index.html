🚀 Future Scope

Expanding the boundaries of what our ISL AI can understand and help translate.

🔄 Text to Sign Translation

Reversing the paradigm. In the future, we aim to allow users to type or speak natural language, and the system will generate an animated avatar performing the corresponding ISL signs.

🌍 Different Native Languages

While our grammar correction and translation currently supports English and Hindi, future architectures will integrate multi-lingual pipelines for seamless translation into regional dialects and international languages.

😊 Facial Expression Recognition

Sign language is more than just hands; facial expressions denote grammar, tone, and questions. By integrating face mesh analysis alongside hand tracking, the AI will contextualize emotions for far more accurate linguistic translation.

ℹ️ About Us

"Breaking silence, building bridges."

❓ Why we are doing this

Communication is a fundamental human right. Millions of speech and hearing-impaired individuals face daily socio-economic barriers due to language disconnects. Bridging the gap shouldn't require expensive hardware or hiring full-time interpreters.

🤝 How it will help

By placing real-time, lightweight AI translation on everyday web browsers, we foster true inclusion in workplaces, schools, hospitals, and homes. Immediate, frictionless communication empowers independence and normalizes sign language interaction globally.

⚙️ Project Details & Architecture

Under the hood of the Indian Sign Language Detector.

🛠 Tools & Technologies

TensorFlow & Keras Google MediaPipe Flask Websockets OpenCV Gemini-2.0-Flash LLM

📊 The Custom Dataset

Generic datasets lack the nuances needed for ISL. We built a custom data tool (smart_capture.py) that records hundreds of frames per sign, automatically tracking hands, tossing out empty frames, and generating robust data points for both 1-hand and 2-hand gestures.

🧠 The Pipeline

  1. Capture: Browser webcam stream.
  2. Track: MediaPipe extracts 3D wrist/finger landmarks.
  3. Engineer: Compute advanced math properties (e.g., Euclidean distances, finger curl angles).
  4. Predict: A lightweight TFLite deep neural network classifies the exact gesture.
  5. NLP Fix: The Gemini AI transforms clunky literal words into fluent, grammatically correct sentences.
